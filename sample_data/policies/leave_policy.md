# Leave & Time-Off Policy

## Section 1: Paid Time Off (PTO)

### 1.1 Annual PTO Allocation
- All full-time employees receive a base of 22 PTO days per year.
- After 3 years of service, employees receive an additional 3 days (25 total).
- After 5 years of service, employees receive an additional 5 days (30 total).
- Managers and directors receive a minimum of 28 PTO days regardless of tenure.

### 1.2 PTO Carryover
- Employees may carry over a maximum of 5 unused PTO days to the next calendar year.
- Carried-over days must be used within the first quarter (Q1) of the new year.
- Any unused carried-over days expire on March 31st.
- There is no cash-out option for unused PTO days.

### 1.3 PTO Request Process
- PTO requests must be submitted at least 5 business days in advance.
- Requests of 5+ consecutive days require manager approval.
- Requests during peak periods (December, end of fiscal year) are subject to team capacity.
- Emergency PTO may be granted retroactively with manager approval.

## Section 2: Sick Leave

### 2.1 Sick Leave Allocation
- All employees receive 10 paid sick days per year.
- Sick days do not carry over to the next year.
- Unused sick days are not compensated.

### 2.2 Medical Documentation
- Sick leave of 3+ consecutive days requires a medical certificate.
- The certificate must be submitted within 48 hours of return to work.

## Section 3: Parental Leave

### 3.1 Maternity Leave
- 14 weeks of fully paid maternity leave.
- Additional 2 weeks unpaid upon request.
- Job protection guaranteed for 6 months post-return.

### 3.2 Paternity Leave
- 5 days of fully paid paternity leave.
- Must be taken within 30 days of the child's birth.

## Section 4: Other Leave Types

### 4.1 Bereavement Leave
- 5 days for immediate family (spouse, children, parents, siblings).
- 2 days for extended family.

### 4.2 Marriage Leave
- 3 days of paid leave for the employee's own marriage.

### 4.3 Study Leave
- Up to 10 days per year for approved professional development.
- Requires written pre-approval from department head.
