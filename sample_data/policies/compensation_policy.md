# Compensation & Payroll Policy

## Section 1: Salary Structure

### 1.1 Pay Frequency
- All employees are paid on a monthly basis.
- Pay date is the last business day of each month.
- Monthly gross pay = Annual salary / 12.

### 1.2 Salary Bands by Level
| Level | Title Range | Annual Salary Range (USD) |
|-------|------------|--------------------------|
| L1 | Junior / Associate | $40,000 - $55,000 |
| L2 | Mid-level | $55,000 - $72,000 |
| L3 | Senior | $72,000 - $90,000 |
| L4 | Lead / Manager | $85,000 - $110,000 |
| L5 | Director | $100,000 - $140,000 |
| L6 | VP / C-level | $130,000 - $200,000 |

### 1.3 Salary Reviews
- Annual salary reviews occur in Q1 (January-March).
- Merit increases range from 0% to 8% based on performance.
- Promotion increases are typically 10-15% above the current salary.
- Cost-of-living adjustments are applied company-wide when applicable.

## Section 2: Deductions

### 2.1 Tax Withholding
- Income tax is withheld based on the employee's tax bracket.
- Tax brackets:
  - 0% on the first $10,000
  - 12% on $10,001 - $40,000
  - 17% on $40,001 - $60,000
  - 22% on $60,001 - $85,000
  - 24% on $85,001 - $150,000
  - 32% on amounts above $150,000
- The effective tax rate is calculated using the progressive bracket system.
- Tax deductions are calculated monthly as: (Annual Tax / 12).

### 2.2 Health Insurance
- Employee contribution varies by plan:
  - Individual plan: $250/month
  - Family plan: $350/month
  - Premium family plan: $400/month
- The company covers 70% of the total premium cost.
- The employee-paid amounts listed above are their 30% share.

### 2.3 Retirement Contributions
- Employee contribution: 4% to 8% of gross pay (employee-selected).
- Company match: 100% match up to 5% of gross pay.
- Example: If employee contributes 6%, company contributes 5% (capped at 5%).
- Vesting schedule: 100% after 2 years of service.

### 2.4 Other Deductions
- Professional development fund: $50/month (optional).
- Meal vouchers: $30/month (subsidized by company).
- Transport allowance: $75/month (included in gross, not deducted).

## Section 3: Overtime & Bonuses

### 3.1 Overtime Policy
- Standard work week: 40 hours (Monday-Friday).
- Overtime rate: 1.5x the hourly rate for hours exceeding 40/week.
- Weekend work: 2x the hourly rate.
- Overtime must be pre-approved by the department manager.
- Monthly overtime cap: 20 hours.

### 3.2 Performance Bonuses
- Annual performance bonus: 0% to 15% of annual salary.
- Bonus is based on individual performance rating and company performance.
- Paid in Q1 following the performance year.
- Pro-rated for employees with less than 12 months of service.

### 3.3 Referral Bonus
- $2,000 for successful employee referrals.
- Paid after the referred employee completes 90 days.

## Section 4: Payroll Processing

### 4.1 Net Pay Calculation
Net Pay = Gross Pay - Tax - Health Insurance - Retirement Contribution - Other Deductions

### 4.2 Payroll Approval Workflow
- Individual payroll calculations are auto-approved.
- Batch payroll processing (for entire departments or company) requires manager approval.
- Batch payroll exceeding $50,000 total requires director-level approval.
- All payroll runs are logged in the audit system.

### 4.3 Payslip Distribution
- Electronic payslips are available by the 28th of each month.
- Payslips include: gross pay, all deductions, net pay, year-to-date totals.
