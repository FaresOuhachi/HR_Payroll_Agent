# Compliance & Labor Regulations

## Section 1: Working Hours

### 1.1 Standard Working Hours
- Maximum standard working hours: 40 hours per week.
- Maximum daily working hours: 8 hours.
- Mandatory rest period: minimum 1 hour lunch break.
- Minimum rest between shifts: 11 consecutive hours.

### 1.2 Overtime Regulations
- Maximum overtime: 20 hours per month.
- Maximum overtime: 200 hours per year.
- Overtime must be compensated at minimum 1.5x the regular hourly rate.
- Weekend/holiday overtime must be compensated at minimum 2x the regular rate.
- Mandatory overtime records must be maintained for 5 years.

## Section 2: Minimum Wage

### 2.1 Minimum Wage Requirements
- National minimum wage: $2,500/month ($30,000/year).
- Regional adjustments may apply:
  - Algiers metropolitan area: $2,800/month minimum.
  - Other urban areas: $2,600/month minimum.
  - Rural areas: $2,500/month minimum (national minimum).
- All employees must earn at least the applicable minimum wage.
- Internship stipends must be at least 50% of minimum wage.

### 2.2 Wage Payment Requirements
- Wages must be paid at least once per month.
- Payment must be made by the last business day of the month.
- Late payment penalties: 1.5% interest per week of delay.
- Salary cannot be reduced without written employee consent.

## Section 3: Employment Contracts

### 3.1 Contract Requirements
- All employment relationships must have a written contract.
- Contract must specify: position, salary, working hours, probation period.
- Probation period: maximum 3 months (6 months for management positions).
- Contract amendments require written agreement from both parties.

### 3.2 Termination Rules
- Notice period: minimum 30 days for employees, 60 days for management.
- Severance pay: 1 month salary per year of service (minimum 2 months).
- Wrongful termination: additional 6 months salary as compensation.
- Final pay must include all accrued but unused PTO.

## Section 4: Data Protection

### 4.1 Employee Data Privacy
- Employee personal data must be stored securely (encrypted at rest).
- Access to payroll data is restricted to authorized personnel only.
- Employee salary information is strictly confidential.
- Data retention: employee records must be kept for 10 years after termination.
- Employees have the right to access and correct their personal data.

### 4.2 Payroll Data Handling
- All payroll calculations must be auditable.
- Payroll data must not be shared with unauthorized third parties.
- Automated payroll systems must maintain a complete audit trail.
- Changes to payroll parameters (tax rates, deductions) must be logged.

## Section 5: Equal Employment

### 5.1 Non-Discrimination
- Equal pay for equal work regardless of gender, age, or background.
- Pay gap analysis must be conducted annually.
- Salary adjustments to address disparities must be prioritized.

### 5.2 Workplace Safety
- Employer must provide a safe working environment.
- Mandatory safety training for all new hires.
- Work-related injury compensation: full salary continuation for up to 6 months.
- Return-to-work program required for employees returning from injury leave.
